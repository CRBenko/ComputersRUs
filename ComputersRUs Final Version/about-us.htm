About Us

Computers Are Us

Founded in 2023, computersRus is a retailer of personal computers, IT and components, as well as consumer electronics. We will continue to provide customers with the best products at the best value that the computer retail world can offer.

The first computersRus location established itself in the heart of Ontario, Canada with an initial vision of providing high-quality products for university students, at a student-friendly price.
